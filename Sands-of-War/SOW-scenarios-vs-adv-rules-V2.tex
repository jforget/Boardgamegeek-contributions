=head1 Description

This document gives the cross-reference between scenarios and advanced
rules, without  the need  to leaf  through the  scenario pages  of the
basic booklet and the expansion kit booklet.

=head1 Errata?

For the I<Defence of Snipe>, I<Abu Agheila> and I<Saddam Strikes Back>
scenarios, in  the I<Advanced Rules> paragraph  the Advanced Munitions
entry  does not  list which  specific  munitions are  used. The  force
listings include only smoke salvoes  plus, for I<Saddam Strikes Back>,
DPICM.

Likewise, no scenario  lists which limited visibility  rules are used.
Usually, only  the "limited visibility:  smoke" is used. At  the other
end  of   the  spectrum,  "limited  visibility:   rain"  and  "limited
visibility: mist" are seldom used.

Not an  error: for the  I<Operation Badr>, I<Salamcheh>  and I<Madinah
Ridge> scenarios, the I<Advanced Munitions> entry mentions CBU, yet in
the force listing no artillery can  fire CBU. But the I<Special Rules>
paragraph tells us that the fighter-bombers can drop CBU munitions.

Not an  error, at least  on the game  designer's part: The  I<Into The
Bekaa>  scenario is  a daylight  scenario. Yet,  the Syrian  artillery
support has 6  salvoes ILLUM. Why? Historically, the  Syrians had some
ILLUM ammo available, even if it is completely useless in the scope on
the scenario. At least, players need not learn the ILLUM special rule.
